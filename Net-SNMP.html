Notes

DEPRECATED: The Net-SNMP license id represented the license stack of 9 licenses found at https://net-snmp.sourceforge.io/about/license.html and net-snmp-5.6.2. Since then, more licenses have been added. Retaining this license id could consequently cause confusion and all of the licenses in the stack are already on the SPDX License List, thus can be identified with an SPDX license expression.

The permitted variants can be found in the corresponding regular expression as shown in title text visible by hovering over the red text.
